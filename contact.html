Quick answers

How fast will I get a response?

WhatsApp and the hotline are usually answered within minutes during operating hours. Email replies typically arrive within a few hours.

I left something in a Rydex car. What do I do?

Message us on WhatsApp with your trip details and a description of the item. We'll connect you with your driver to arrange a return.

How do I report a safety concern?

Choose "Safety concern" in the form above or call the hotline directly. Safety reports are prioritised and handled confidentially.

I want to drive with Rydex. Where do I sign up?

Head to the Become a Driver section on our homepage to start your application.
